Thames Valley Bulletin

Bringing facts to the people of the Thames Valley with transparency and clarity

This website aims to bring facts to the people of the Thames Valley without all the conjecture, abuse, PCC crime washing and mis information that comes with social media. The Freedom of Information Act, used in the right way, is a powerful tool that holds those that spend the taxes we give them, to account. However, some public authorities seem to think it does not apply to them. Content will be added as issues arise and may highlight irony so readers can come to their own conclusion over the right or wrong of what we have published.
